Frequently Asked Questions?

Is my money secure?

Yes! We use enterprise-grade encryption, secure PIN hashing, and comply with all financial regulations. Your funds are protected at all times.

What are the minimum and maximum amounts I can save?

There are no strict limits. Here We Allow You To Save Any Amount You Have.

How are funds released to my child?

You set release conditions (age, milestone, or date). When met, funds can be transferred to the child's account or used for approved purposes.

Can my child see the savings?

No! Children have no access to view their savings progress.

What if I forget my Password?

You can reset your password through phone verification. We'll send a reset code to your registered number.

Is There a cost to use MyChild ?

Our basic platform is free to use. Except When Depositing Above 5000 We Take 1% on Deposited Amount.
