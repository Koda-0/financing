Frequently Asked Questions

Is my money secure?

Yes! We use enterprise-grade encryption, secure PIN hashing, and comply with all financial regulations. Your funds are protected at all times.

What are the minimum and maximum amounts I can save?

There are no strict limits. Start with as little as RWF 500 and save as much as you need for your child's future goals.

How are funds released to my child?

You set release conditions (age, milestone, or date). When met, funds can be transferred to the child's account or used for approved purposes.

Can my child see the savings?

Yes! Children have limited access to view their savings progress, which helps them understand the value of long-term planning.

What if I forget my PIN?

You can reset your PIN through phone verification. We'll send a reset code to your registered number.

Is there a cost to use FutureVault?

Our basic platform is free to use. Optional premium features and integrations may have nominal fees.
